Trabel

Collaborative Planning Tool


As travel enthusiasts, we decided to create a trip planning tool that we would enjoy using, without the flaws of the existing ones. We would like not only to plan our own trips but also join other people's trips and plan them with all the trip-mates: cooperatively and in real-time.

Vision

A trip planning platform where people can collaborate in real-time to create, share and explore travel itineraries, destinations and points of interest.
